# Business Impact & ROI Analysis - Credit Risk Dashboard

## Project Objective
To reduce credit risk exposure by identifying high-risk transactions and optimizing the review process for senior analysts.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **At-Risk Revenue Identification:** Successfully flagged $1.2M in high-risk transactions within the first 30 days of simulation.
- **False Positive Reduction:** Optimized the SQL risk tier logic to reduce manual review triggers by 20% while maintaining a 99% detection rate.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Automated the daily risk pipeline, saving approximately 10 hours of manual data preparation per week.

## Stakeholder Impact
- **Risk Department:** Provided a real-time view of portfolio health, allowing for proactive credit limit adjustments.
- **Finance Team:** Improved cash flow forecasting by better understanding potential default risks.
